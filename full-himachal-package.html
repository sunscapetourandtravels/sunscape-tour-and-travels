Experience Himachal

Mountains, Valleys, Monasteries & Himalayan Adventures

Explore the breathtaking beauty of Himachal Pradesh through Shimla, Manali, Dharamshala and Dalhousie. Experience snow-capped mountains, monasteries, pine forests, waterfalls and scenic valleys across one unforgettable Himalayan journey.

From Kufri adventure activities and Solang Valley snow adventures to the spiritual charm of Dharamshala and the peaceful landscapes of Khajjiar, this package offers the complete Himachal experience.

8 Nights / 9 Days Itinerary

Day 01

Delhi To Shimla

Pickup from Delhi Airport / Railway Station / Bus Stand and drive to Shimla. En route visit Pinjor Garden and Hanuman Temple. Arrival at Shimla and overnight stay.

Delhi Pickup Dinner Included
Day 02

Shimla Local Sightseeing & Kufri

Visit Kufri Fun World, Nature Park, Kalibari Mata Temple, Mall Road, Scandal Point, Tibetan Market and Lakkar Bazaar.

Kufri Mall Road Breakfast & Dinner
Day 03

Shimla To Manali

Scenic drive to Manali via Sundernagar Lake, Mini Kashi Mandi, Pandoh Dam, Aut Tunnel and Kullu Valley.

Kullu Valley Pandoh Dam Breakfast & Dinner
Day 04

Manali Local Sightseeing

Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Tibetan Monastery, Club House, Vashisht Temple, Van Vihar and Mall Road Manali.

Hadimba Temple Mall Road Monastery
Day 05

Solang Valley, Atal Tunnel & Sissu

Explore Solang Valley and enjoy adventure activities including Paragliding, Ropeway and Skiing. Later drive through Atal Tunnel to Sissu village.

Paragliding Atal Tunnel Sissu
Day 06

Manali To Dharamshala

Morning drive from Manali to Dharamshala. Check-in at hotel and relax. Dinner and overnight stay at Dharamshala.

Scenic Drive Breakfast & Dinner
Day 07

Dharamshala Sightseeing & Dalhousie

Visit Dalai Lama Temple, Bhagsunag Waterfall, HPCA Cricket Stadium and Dal Lake. Later drive to Dalhousie for overnight stay.

Dalai Lama Temple Bhagsunag Dalhousie
Day 08

Dalhousie & Khajjiar Sightseeing

Visit Khajjiar Lake, Khajji Nag Temple, Panchpula, Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary, St. Francis Church and Dainkund Peak.

Khajjiar Dainkund Peak Kalatop
Day 09

Dalhousie To Delhi Drop

After breakfast checkout from hotel. Cab transfer to Delhi Airport, Railway Station or Bus Stand.

Breakfast Included Drop Included
