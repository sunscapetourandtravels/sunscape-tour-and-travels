Privacy Policy

Your privacy and personal information matter to us. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your data when you use our website or enquire about our travel services.

Last Updated • May 2026

1. Introduction

Sunscape Tour and Travels is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors, travellers and customers using our website and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store and use your personal information when you interact with our company through bookings, enquiries, WhatsApp, email communication or website forms.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and travel-related information including:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Travel Preferences & Destinations
  • Pickup & Drop Details
  • Booking Requirements
  • Hotel & Package Preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to provide better travel services and booking assistance. Your information may be used for:

  • Tour package bookings and confirmations
  • Customer support and travel assistance
  • Sending itinerary updates and travel details
  • Improving our website and travel experiences
  • Responding to enquiries and requests

4. Payment & Booking Information

Payment-related information may be processed through trusted third-party providers or direct communication channels. We do not intentionally store sensitive banking information on our website servers.

5. Information Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative precautions to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, misuse, loss or disclosure. However, no online transmission method can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Our services may involve third-party travel partners including hotels, transportation providers, payment gateways or booking services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external services or third-party websites.

7. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to improve user experience, website performance and visitor understanding. Cookies help us personalize content and enhance browsing functionality.

8. Promotional Communication

We may occasionally contact users regarding offers, travel packages or promotional updates. You may request to stop receiving promotional communication at any time.

9. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for providing accurate personal and travel information during bookings and enquiries. Incorrect details may affect bookings, transportation or hotel arrangements.

10. Policy Updates

Sunscape Tour and Travels reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Updated versions will be published on this page.

11. Contact Information

For questions, concerns or privacy-related requests, please contact Sunscape Tour and Travels through our official website, email or WhatsApp support.
