Experience Himachal

Snowy Mountains, Valleys & Himalayan Adventures

Discover the beauty of Himachal Pradesh with our Shimla and Manali holiday package. Experience snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, colonial streets, pine forests and thrilling adventure activities while travelling through the heart of the Himalayas.

From the peaceful charm of Shimla to the adventure-filled valleys of Manali, this journey offers breathtaking landscapes, temples, local markets and unforgettable mountain experiences for couples, families and groups.

5 Nights / 6 Days Itinerary

Day 01

Delhi To Shimla

Pickup from Delhi Airport / Railway Station / Bus Stand by exclusive vehicle and drive to Shimla. En route visit Pinjor Garden and Hanuman Temple. On arrival check into hotel and relax. Dinner and overnight stay at Shimla.

Delhi Pickup Dinner Included
Day 02

Shimla Local Sightseeing & Kufri

After breakfast proceed for Shimla and Kufri sightseeing. Visit Kufri Fun World, Nature Park, Mall Road, Scandal Point, Kalibari Mata Temple, Tibetan Market and Lakkar Bazaar. Dinner and overnight stay at Shimla.

Kufri Mall Road Breakfast & Dinner
Day 03

Shimla To Manali

After breakfast checkout from hotel and drive to Manali. En route sightseeing includes Sundernagar Lake, Mini Kashi Mandi, Pandoh Dam, Aut Tunnel and Kullu Valley. Arrival at Manali and overnight stay.

Kullu Valley Pandoh Dam Breakfast & Dinner
Day 04

Manali Local Sightseeing

Explore Hadimba Devi Temple, Tibetan Monastery, Club House, Vashisht Temple, Van Vihar and Mall Road Manali. Return to hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Hadimba Temple Mall Road Monastery
Day 05

Solang Valley & Anjani Mahadev

Visit Solang Valley and enjoy adventure activities including Paragliding, Ropeway, Skiing and Zorbing at your own cost. Later visit Anjani Mahadev Temple, famous for its giant ice Shivling. Dinner and overnight stay at Manali.

Paragliding Ropeway Snow Activities
Day 06

Manali To Delhi Drop

After breakfast checkout from hotel. Cab transfer to Delhi Airport, Railway Station or Bus Stand. Tour ends with beautiful memories.

Breakfast Included Drop Included
